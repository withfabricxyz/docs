# Dashboard

The Hypersub Dashboard is central to understanding your membership. Gain an overview of your income, manage members, and withdraw funds all from one place.

To get started, make sure you are connected. From anywhere, click the button next to your avatar to open the menu and select Dashboard.

### Stats

Membership stats takes a look at the overall health of your Hypersub. View member count, monthly recurring revenue, churn, retention, lapse, and renewal rates. Click the filter to switch date ranges. Hide stats by clicking the eye icon button.

Get paid from your membership by withdrawing available funds. Click the Withdraw button to review. Important: make sure the recipient account is correct as it is where the available funds will be sent. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction. It may take a few seconds to receive your funds.

If an alternative revenue recipient is set you will see a Transfer button. Transfer works the same as withdraw, except funds are sent to another specified account or address instead. Learn more about revenue recipients in [dashboard settings.](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/dashboard_settings)

If [card payments](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/cards) are set up, you get paid in fiat separately through your Stipe dashboard.

### Import

Import your audience from other platforms or memberships. Click the Import button and enter your accounts. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction. Once complete, your membership is populated with your audience. Learn more about [grants.](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/grants)

### Export

Export a list of your membership for external use. Click the Export button, select status and enter time purchased to filter members. Click the Download button to save a CSV to your device.

Export can also be used for member raffles. Export random accounts by switching it on and entering the number of accounts pulled. Winners are displayed in the preview below.

### Members

Take closer look at your Hypersub audience from the members table. View individual member infomation in each row, with important details like account and email. Click the filter button to view members based on status such as active or churned.

Creators on Hypersub always have access to member emails.

Remove members by kicking them from your Hypersub. To kick a member click the more icon, then click kick from the menu. Select a refund option if the member has a remaining balance. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction.

Kick can be useful for removing inactive members to create openings in a supply cap, or for removing individials due to misconduct within a community guideline.
