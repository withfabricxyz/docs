# Dashboard

The Hypersub Dashboard is central to understanding your subscription. Gain an overview of your income, manage subscribers, and withdraw funds all from one place.

To get started, make sure you are connected. From anywhere, click the button next to your avatar to open the menu and select Dashboard.

### Stats

Subscription stats takes a look at the overall health of your subscription. View subscriber count, monthly recurring revenue, churn, retention, lapse, and renewal rates. Click the filter to switch date ranges. Hide stats by clicking the eye icon button.

Get paid from your subscription by withdrawing available funds. Click the Withdraw button to review. Important: make sure the recipient address is correct as it is where the available funds will be sent. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction. It may take a few seconds to receive your funds.

If an alternative revenue recipient is set you will see a Transfer button. Transfer works the same as withdraw, except funds are sent to another specified address instead. Learn more about revenue recipients in [dashboard settings.](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/dashboard_settings.)

If [card payments](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/cards) are set up, you get paid through your Stipe dashboard.

### Import

Import your audience from other platforms or subscriptions. Click the Import button and enter your addresses. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction. Once complete, your subscription is populated with your audience. Learn more about [grants.](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/grants)

### Export

Export a list of your subscribers for external use. Click the Export button, select status and enter time purchased to filter subscribers. Click the Download button to save a CSV to your device.

Export can also be used for subscriber raffles. Export random addresses by switching it on and entering the number of addresses pulled. Winners are displayed in the preview below.

### Subscribers

Take closer look at your Hypersub audience from the subscriber table. View individual subscriber infomation in each row, with important details like address and email. Click the filter button to view subscribers based on status such as active or churned.

Creators on Hypersub always have access to subscriber emails.

Remove subscribers by kicking them from your Hypersub. To kick a subscriber click the more icon, then click kick from the menu. Select a refund option if the subscriber has a remaining balance. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction.

Kick can be useful for removing inactive subscribers to create openings in a supply cap, or for removing individials due to misconduct within community guidelines.
