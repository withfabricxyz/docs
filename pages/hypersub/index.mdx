# Home Page

Welcome to Hypersub. The home page is where you can browse subscriptions to get a feel for a variety of creator offerings.

Connect your wallet using the button at the top right of the page. Next, you will be asked for an email, which is necessary for subscription notifications and is required to subscribe. Your email will only be shared with creators you are subscribed to. Verify by entering the six-digit code sent to your email. You are now ready to explore Hypersub and subscribe.
