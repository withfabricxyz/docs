Verify the amount of time a subscriber has left on your Hypersub.

1. Copy the address you want to verify
2. Go to your subscription page and click Details
3. Click NFT subscription contract to open BaseScan
4. Click the Contract tab, then click the Read Contract as Proxy tab
5. Expand the balanceOf section, and paste in the address
6. Click the Query button, copy the numSeconds value
7. Open a new tab, paste the value, and append with 'seconds to months' (or weeks, quarters, years)
