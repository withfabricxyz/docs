[Deform](https://www.deform.cc/) allows creators to collect verified info and more via forms and campaigns.

### Create a Form

1. Connect to Deform and create your workspace
2. Create a new form by clicking the Blank button or starting with a template
3. Add an image and title, edit details and add questions
4. The form is live upon creation, share the link with your members

We only cover the basics here, Deform has a wide array of applications that this guide does not begin to describe. [Learn more](https://docs.deform.cc/)

Create a members only group chat on [Warpcast](https://web.warpcast.com/).

### Contract Address

To enable a token-gated group chat you'll need to provide your Hypersub contract address.

1. Go to your Hypersub Profile and click the Details button
2. Find your NFT subscription contract and click to open BaseScan
3. Click the Copy address button

### Create Group

1. Log in to Warpcast and click the [Direct Casts tab](https://warpcast.com/~/inbox)
2. Click the New direct cast button and then click Create new group
3. Enter your group details and click Create group
4. Click the ••• icon to open Details and then click the settings icon
5. Scroll down to Must hold NFT and switch it on
6. Paste in your contract address prepended by eip155:8453: and save
7. Copy the Share invite link and add it to your Hypersub description

Switch on the Check for NFT setting to remove inactive members from your group an a daily basis.

Learn more about [token-gated group chats](https://warpcast.notion.site/NFT-gated-group-chats-0f76abc16d344414b42fcfea5e55f86d).
