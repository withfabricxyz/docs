Reward members who share your Hypersub with referral links. Members that share links leading to successful conversions are given a percentage of that membership's revenue for that purchase.

To get started, go to your Dashboard and click the Referrals tab.

### Create Referral

1. Click the Create button, enter a title and select a percentage
2. Review, confirm and sign the transaction

You must create and share a referral link with each member in order for them to receive referral rewards.

### Create Link

Create and share referral links with members.

1. Click the more icon in the referral's table row, then click Create Link in the menu
2. Enter the member's account and Click the Create button to generate the link
3. Copy the link and share it with the member

### Edit 

Referrals can be edited at any time.

1. Click the more icon in the referral's table row, then click Edit in the menu.
2. Change the title, percentage or other advanced options
3. Confirm and sign the transaction

Delete a referral by clicking Delete in the menu and confirming.

## Advanced

Access advanced referral options by clicking the Advanced button.

### Default Link

Share a default referral link with each new member that joins your membership.

1. Set a link to default by switching it on

Members can always access the default link from the Earn tab on your membership profile.

### Permanent Link

Referral links can be made permanent, meaning they can never be edited or deleted.

1. Set your link to permenent by switching it on

This setting has a strong effect and allows a member to make referrals indefinitely.

### Single Account

Create a referral link that is only for one member.

1. Set your link to single account by switching it on
2. Enter an account

Keep track of who has what referral. Allows bespoke referral links for creators with more advanced referral structures.
