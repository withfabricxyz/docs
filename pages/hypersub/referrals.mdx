Reward subscribers who share your Hypersub with referral links. Subscribers that share links leading to successful conversions are compensated with a percentage of that subscription's revenue.

To get started, go to your dashboard and click the Referrals tab.

### Create

Click the Create button, enter a title for your referral and select a percentage. Confirm to open your wallet and sign the transaction. Next you will need to assign the link and share with subscribers.

### Assign

Assign referral links to subscribers. Click the more icon in the referral's table row. Click Create Link in the menu. Enter the subscriber's address and Click the Create button to generate the link. Copy the link and share it with the subscriber.

### Edit

Referrals can be edited at any time. Click the more icon in the referral's table row. Click Edit in the menu. Change the title, percentage and other options covered below. Confirm and sign.

Delete a referral by clicking Delete in the menu and confirming.

### Default Link

Assign a link to each new subscriber. Set a link to default by switching it on. After time is purchased subscribers are shown their default referral link.

Subscribers can always access the default link from the Earn tab on your subscription page.

### Single Address

Create a referral link that is only for one address. Set your link to single address by switching it on and entering an address. Single address referrals are automatically assigned to the entered address.

Keep track of who has what referral. Allows bespoke referrals for creators with more advanced referral structures.

### Permanent Link

Referral links can be made permanent, meaning they can never be edited or deleted. Set your link to permenent by switching it on. This setting has a strong effect and gives a subscriber the ability make referrals indefinitely so long as they have the link.
