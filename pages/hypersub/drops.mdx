Airdrop exclusively to members without your work going up for sale.

### Create Mint Page

To get started go to [highlight.xyz](https://www.highlight.xyz/) and sign in.

1. Click the Create button and select Edition
2. Select Mainnet for your Network and Base for your Blockchain
3. Upload your media, enter the details, and set the end date to today
4. Click Deploy collection and sign the transaction
5. Click Manage collection and copy your mint page contract address

Setting the duration to 1 minute does not effect how members receive your airdrop. It limits exposure to non-members finding your page in the window of time before you airdrop. Once the airdrop is complete, your work is unavailable to the public.

### Airdrop from Highlight

Deliver your work directly to members from the contract level.

1. Go to [BaseScan](https://basescan.org/) and paste in your mint page contract address
2. Click the Contract tab and then click the Write Contract as Proxy tab
3. Click the Connect to Web3 button and sign the transaction
4. Expand 9. registerMinter, paste in your Ethereum account, and click Write
5. Expand 8. mintOneToRecipients and enter 0 for the editionId
6. Go to your Hypersub dashboard, click Export, and click the copy icon
7. Go to BaseScan and paste in your members accounts, comma separated
8. Click Write and sign the transaction

**Important:** for steps 3 and 4 be sure to use the same account as the one used when creating your mint page.

### Drop on Hypersub

Notify members about your airdrop, and present your work to the world.

1. Go to the Mint page on Highlight you airdropped from and copy the url
2. Go to your membership page on Hypersub and click the Drops tab
3. Click Create drop and paste in your Claim page url
4. Click Create to save and notify your members

**Important:** your airdrop has already occurred from Highlight, dropping on Hypersub does not airdrop again.

### Create Claim Page

To get started go to [manifold.xyz](https://www.manifold.xyz/) and log in.

1. Click the Create button and select Claim page
2. Click New Contract and enter details matching your tier, and click Next
3. Upload your media, enter the details, click Save then Next
4. Enter the details of your mint page, click Next
5. Enter your mint rules. **Important:** set the Total Supply to Limited: 1. Click Next
6. Do not edit your Audience settings, click Next
7. Review, click Publish and confirm the transaction - this may take a few seconds

Setting the supply limit to 1 does not effect how members receive your airdrop. It limits exposure to non-members finding your page in the window before you airdrop. Once the airdrop is complete, your work is listed as sold out to the public.

### Create List

Create a members only account list for your airdrop. **Important:** be sure to end your file name with .csv before you save.

1. Go to your Hypersub dashboard and click Export
2. Scroll to the account area and click the copy icon
3. Open TextEdit or similar application, create a new file, and paste your member accounts
4. Append each account with ,1 and save

### Airdrop from Manifold

Deliver your work directly to members.

1. Go to your Creations in Manifold and click the work you want to airdrop
2. Click the More Options tab and upload your CSV
3. Click Begin Airdrop and confirm the transaction

### Drop on Hypersub

Notify members about your airdrop, and present your work to the world.

1. Go to the Claim page on Manifold you airdropped from and copy the url
2. Go to your dashboard on Hypersub and click the Drops tab
3. Click Create drop and paste in your Claim page url
4. Click Create to save and notify your members

**Important:** your airdrop has already occurred from Manifold, dropping on Hypersub does not airdrop again.

To publish on PODS you must first [apply as a creator](https://pods-media.typeform.com/to/QzqAtlKX?typeform-source=pods.media).

### Create Episode

To get started go to [pods.media](https://pods.media/) and log in.

1. From your dashboard click the New Episode button
2. Enter your episode title and description, upload your cover art and audio file
3. Enter the Publication Date, Episode Number and Credits
4. Set your Price, Supply, Release date and Duration
5. Click Continue and sign the transaction

### Drop on Hypersub

Deliver your work directly to members.

1. Go your Episode page on PODS and copy the url
2. Go to your dashboard on Hypersub and click the Drops tab
3. Click Create drop and paste in your Episode page url
4. Click Create to airdrop to members. Sign the transaction

**Important:** when the drop is created you'll be prompted to airdrop.

To get started go to [sound.xyz](https://www.sound.xyz/) and log in.

### Artist Application

To publish on Sound.xyz you must apply as an Artist.

1. Click your avatar menu, then click For artists
2. Enter your artist name, handle, location, and avatar
3. Link your socials and Spotify, then click Submit

### Create Release

1. Click the Create button, then click Upload sound
2. Click the Advanced Upload Tool link
3. Upload your song file and artwork, then enter the title, genre and description. Click Save

### Create List

Create a members only account list for your release. **Important:** be sure to end your file name with .csv before you save.

1. Go to your Hypersub dashboard and click Export
2. Scroll to the account area and click the copy icon
3. Open TextEdit or similar application, create a new file, paste your member accounts and save

### Drop Configuration

Release music exclusively to your members.

1. Switch Free Edition off, and set your Duration
2. **Important:** set Open Edition Price to 69 ETH. This price deters non-members from collecting.
3. Switch Presale on and set your Start time to now and your Presale Price to 0 ETH
4. **Important:** remove All my collectors from Allowlist
5. Upload your members CSV, click Continue
6. Review your release, click Continue and sign the transaction

### Drop on Hypersub

Deliver your work directly to members.

1. Go your Release page on Sound.xyz and copy the url
2. Go to your Dashboard and click the Drops tab
3. Click Create Drop and paste in your Episode page url
4. Click Create to airdrop to members. Sign the transaction

**Important:** when the drop is created you'll be prompted to airdrop.

To get started go to [zora.co](https://zora.co/) and log in.

### Create Post

1. Click the Plus icon button and upload your art
2. Enter your title and description
3. Click the Post button and sign the transaction

By default, creators receive 10M coins from each post. If you want to increase your initial holding for airdrop purposes, you must buy more of your coins before posting. Learn more about [Coins](https://support.zora.co/en/articles/4653825).

### Airdrop Coins

Airdrop Coins to your members with [Gaslite](https://drop.gaslite.org/).

1. Go to your post and click the Details tab
2. Copy your post's contract address and go to Gaslite
3. Make sure you connected to Base and paste in your contract address
4. Go to your Dashboard, click the Members tab, then click the Export button
5. Copy your member's addresses and go back to Gaslite
6. Paste in your addresses, appended with a comma and the amount of Coins you wish to airdrop. **Example:** 0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000, 1000000
7. Click the Continue button and sign the transactions

### Drop on Hypersub

Deliver your work directly to members.

1. Go your post on Zora and copy the url
2. Go to your Dashboard on Hypersub and click the Drops tab
3. Click Create Drop, paste in your post url, and click Create
