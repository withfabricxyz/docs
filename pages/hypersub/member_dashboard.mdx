Access your memberships all in one place:

- Add time
- Refer and earn
- View past drops
- Gift memberships

To get started, click the home icon from anywhere.

## Memberships

View all active memberships from the side bar. Any inactive memberships will be hidden in the archive. Click the Show archive button to view.

### Feed

Your feed is where drops arrive, notifying you about creative work sent to your wallet. In other cases you may see rewards deposits, event invitations, forms or otherwise. Click the image to view the drop's origin.

### Tier Card

The tier card shows you how much time is left on your membership, and any rewards you have accrued over time. Click the Add time button to purchase more time or send a gift by clicking Gift membership.

### Refer and Earn

Share referral links and earn. Any new memberships purchased with your referral link earns you a percentage of the purchase amount. Click the social buttons to share, or copy the link.
