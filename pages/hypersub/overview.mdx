# Hypersub

What is Hypersub good for?
1. Earning recurring revenue
2. Distributing your work
3. Building a community

Hypersub is an STP-based client enabling onchain membership for artists, communities, software businesses, and more.

Each Hypersub membership token is an [ERC-721](https://www.coinbase.com/learn/crypto-glossary/what-is-erc-721) NFT. It can be used across numerous tools and platforms to unlock perks, setup community spaces, provide gated access to your community, help understand their interests, and more.

To learn more about STP – the protocol underlying Hypersub – see the [protocol documentation](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/stp/overview).
