# Members

Manage your membership by viewing member count, churn and retention rates, and other important member details.

To get started, go to your Dashboard and click the Members tab.

## Import

Creators new to Hypersub can use grants to import their audience from other platforms or memberships.

1. Click the Import button and enter your accounts
2. Confirm to and sign the transaction.

Once completed, your membership is populated with your audience. Learn more about [Grants](https://docs.withfabric.xyz/hypersub/grants).

## Export

Export a list of your membership for external use. 

1. Click the Export button, select status and enter time purchased to filter members
2. Click the Download button to save a CSV to your device

Export can be used to determine random accounts for member raffles:

1. Click the Export button and switch Randomize on
2. Enter the number of randomized accounts. Winners are displayed in the preview below

## Member Table

Take closer look at your Hypersub audience from the member table. View important member details like account and email. Click the filter button to view members based on status.

Creators on Hypersub always have access to member emails.

Remove members by kicking them from your Hypersub.

1. Click the ••• icon in the member's table row, then click Kick member
2. Select a refund option if the member has any remaining time
3. Confirm and sign the transaction

Kick can be useful for removing inactive members to create openings in a supply cap, or for removing individials due to misconduct within community guidelines.
