### 2. Customize Profile
<video controls width="100%" style={{borderRadius: "12px", margin: "1em 0"}}><source src="https://storage.withfabric.xyz/videos/02-create-profile.mp4" type="video/mp4" /></video>
1. Click the icon center-top of the page
2. Click the profile photo
3. Select a photo from your device
4. Click the auto generated name
5. Enter your desired display name

### 3. Create Campaign
<video controls width="100%" style={{borderRadius: "12px", margin: "1em 0"}}><source src="https://storage.withfabric.xyz/videos/03-create.mp4" type="video/mp4" /></video>
1. From the Fabric [home page](https://crowdfi.withfabric.xyz) click *Create* or navigate to https://crowdfi.withfabric.xyz/create
2. Enter a campaign name
3. Enter a campaign description
4. Upload photos (optional)
5. Specify a funding goal
6. Specify campaign duration
7. Enter a recipient address
8. Randomize campaign art (optional)
9. Click *Preview*
10. Click *Deploy*

### 4. Post Update
<video controls width="100%" style={{borderRadius: "12px", margin: "1em 0"}}><source src="https://storage.withfabric.xyz/videos/07-update.mp4" type="video/mp4" /></video>
1. On the campaign page, click *Post Update*
2. Enter desired text into the input field
3. Click *Post Update* to submit
4. To cancel, click the *Cancel* button

### 5. Transfer Funds
<video controls width="100%" style={{borderRadius: "12px", margin: "1em 0"}}><source src="https://storage.withfabric.xyz/videos/08-transfer.mp4" type="video/mp4" /></video>
1. On the campaign page, click *Transfer Funds*
2. Review the amount and double-check the recipient address then click *Confirm*
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet

### 6. Deposit Yield
<video controls width="100%" style={{borderRadius: "12px", margin: "1em 0"}}><source src="https://storage.withfabric.xyz/videos/09-distribute-yield.mp4" type="video/mp4" /></video>
1. On the campaign page, click *Deposit Yield*
2. Enter the amount you wish to deposit into the crowdfinancing contract, then click *Approve*
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
